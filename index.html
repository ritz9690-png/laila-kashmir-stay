About Laila Kashmiri Stay

A family-run paying guest house in the heart of Srinagar, just minutes from Dal Lake. We started Laila Stay with one goal — give travellers the warmth of a Kashmiri home along with the comfort of a proper hotel. In partnership with Voyara Trails, we also help guests plan the rest of their Kashmir trip, from shikara rides to Gulmarg snowfields.
