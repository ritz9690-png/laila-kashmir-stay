About Laila Kashmiri Stay

A family-run paying guest house in the heart of Srinagar, just minutes from Dal Lake. We started Laila Stay with one goal — give travellers the warmth of a Kashmiri home along with the comfort of a proper hotel. In partnership with Voyara Trails, we also help guests plan the rest of their Kashmir trip — shikara rides on Dal Lake, day trips to Pahalgam and Gulmarg, and everything in between. Whether you're here for a quiet weekend or a full Kashmir holiday, our family looks after every detail so you don't have to.
